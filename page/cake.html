CAKE RECIPE

This recipe features layers of moist chocolate cake soaked with rich cherry syrup, filled with whipped cream and cherries, and garnished with chocolate shavings.

Reference video:

Ingredients

Chocolate cake
1 1/2 cups flour, 2 cups sugar, ¾ cup cocoa powder, 1 tsp baking powder, 2 tsp baking soda, 1 cup buttermilk, ½ cup oil, 2 eggs, 1 cup brewed coffee, 1 tsp vanilla.
Cherry filling: 1 can cherry pie filling, ½ cup cherry juice.
Whipped cream: 1½ cups cream, 3 tbsp sugar, ½ tsp vanilla.

Preparation

1. Make the Cake: Preheat oven to 175°C. Mix dry and wet ingredients separately, combine, pour into pans, and bake until done.

2. Prepare Cherry Filling: Mix cherry filling with juice. Use fresh cherries if available.

3. Whipped Cream: Beat chilled cream with sugar and vanilla until stiff peaks form.
