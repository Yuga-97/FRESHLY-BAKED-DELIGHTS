COOKIE RECIPE

Reference video:

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (120g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup (100g) sugar
  • 1/2 cup (100g) brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup (115g) butter (softened)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips (optional)

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, cream butter and sugars until smooth.
  4. Add egg and vanilla, and mix well.
  5. Gradually add dry ingredients into wet mixture.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Scoop dough onto a baking tray lined with parchment paper.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden brown.
  9. Cool on a wire rack and enjoy!
