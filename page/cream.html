CREAM BUN RECIPE

This recipe features layers of moist chocolate cake soaked with rich cherry syrup, filled with whipped cream and cherries, and garnished with chocolate shavings.

Reference video:

Ingredients (for 6 buns)

  • 2 cups All-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp Sugar
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Instant yeast
  • 3/4 cup Warm milk
  • 2 tbsp Butter (softened)
  • 1 cup Whipped cream (sweetened)
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla essence (optional)

Preparation Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, salt, and yeast in a bowl.
  2. Add warm milk gradually and knead into a soft dough.
  3. Add butter and knead for 8-10 minutes until smooth.
  4. Cover and let it rise for 1 to 1.5 hours (until doubled).
  5. Divide into 6 balls and shape into oval buns.
  6. Place on a tray, cover, and rest for 20-30 minutes.
  7. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 15-18 minutes until golden.
  8. Cool the buns, slice in the middle (not fully).
  9. Fill with whipped cream.
  10. Dust with powdered sugar and enjoy!
