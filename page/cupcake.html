CUPCAKE RECIPE

Reference video:

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Preparation

  1. Preheat Oven: Set oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.
  2. Melt Butter: In a medium saucepan, melt the butter. Remove from heat and stir in sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. Mix Dry Ingredients: Beat in cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder.
  4. Bake: Spread batter into prepared pan. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes. Do not overcook.
  5. Cool & Serve: Let it cool before cutting into squares. Enjoy!
