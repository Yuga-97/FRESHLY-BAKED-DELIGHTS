DONUTS

Reference video:

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup butter (softened)
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • Oil for frying
  • Powdered sugar or glaze for topping

Preparation

  1. Dissolve yeast in warm water and let it sit for 5-10 minutes.
  2. Mix in milk, sugar, salt, egg, butter, and half the flour.
  3. Add the rest of the flour and knead into a soft dough.
  4. Cover and let it rise for 1 hour until doubled.
  5. Roll out the dough and cut into donut shapes.
  6. Let the donuts rise again for 30 minutes.
  7. Heat oil and fry donuts until golden brown on both sides.
  8. Drain on paper towels and coat with sugar or glaze.
