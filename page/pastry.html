Cake Pastry Recipe

Reference video:

Ingredients

  • Cake Pastry :
  • Flour:All-purpose flour (maida) is a common base.
  • Sweetener: Sugar or powdered sugar adds sweetness and aids in browning.
  • Fat: Butter or vegetable oil provides moisture and tenderness. .
  • Liquid: Milk, water, or even fruit juice can be used to provide the necessary moisture. .
  • Leavening Agents: Baking soda and baking powder are crucial for creating a light and fluffy texture, Bake with Shivesh explains.

    • Egg Replacements:

  • Yogurt or Curd: Adds moisture and a slight tang, Bake with Shivesh notes.
  • Vinegar or Lemon Juice: For classic vanilla cakes.

    Flavorings:

  • Vanilla Extract:For classic vanilla cakes.
  • Cocoa Powder: For chocolate cakes.
  • Orange Zest or Spices: Can add additional flavor notes.
  • Salts: A small amount of salt enhances the overall flavor.

Preparation

  1. In a bowl, mix flour and salt.
  2. Add cold butter cubes and mix lightly (butter should remain visible).
  3. Pour in cold water gradually and form a rough dough.
  4. Shape into a rectangle, wrap in plastic, and chill for 30 minutes.
  5. Roll dough into a long rectangle, fold into thirds like a letter.
  6. Turn 90°, roll again, and fold. Repeat 4-5 times, chilling if needed.
  7. Wrap and refrigerate 1 hour before using.
  8. Bake filled pastries at 200°C (400°F) until golden brown.
